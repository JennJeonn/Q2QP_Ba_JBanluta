CRIMES AGAINST CHILDREN

ORGANIZATION BACKGROUND

Save the Children is an organization aimed on championing the rights of 2.3 billion children. They work to deliver and inspire programming and advocacy for children in 120 countries.

What we have accomplished so far

1. Save the Children does whatever it takes to be there for children in crisis

2. Helped 118 million children, in 116 countries, become healthier, safer and better educated.

3. Partnered with governments and local organizations to provide immediate assistance when an emergency hits.

4. Worked with communities to prepare for and mitigate the impact of disasters.
